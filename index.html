How to Join the Community

To join the SuperiorSkygen community, follow these simple steps:

  1. Add EGNET500 as a friend on Xbox Live.
  2. Launch Minecraft Bedrock Edition on your device.
  3. Go to your Friends List within Minecraft.
  4. You should see "SuperiorSkygen" listed among your friends.
  5. Click on "SuperiorSkygen" to join the server and start playing!
